# Mitchell Forbes Landing Page | Design Handover Brief

Follow these specifications to recreate or extend the Mitchell Forbes landing page identity.

## 1. Aesthetic Baseline
- **Primary Reference**: [michael-paulk.gomitch.com](https://michael-paulk.gomitch.com/)
- **Style Concept**: "Quiet Luxury" / Ultra-Minimalist.
- **Vibe**: High-contrast (Black/White), cinematic, and high-authority.

## 2. Global Branding & Assets
- **Logo**: Use `New Mitchell Forbes Simplified.svg`. It is mathematically centered and aligned for a premium feel.
- **Logo Treatment**: Use inverted white on dark backgrounds; original black on light backgrounds.
- **Imagery**: Cinematic, wide-angle luxury architecture. (Refer to `Lane_Branding_Reference\Photos\1.png`).

## 3. Typography & Colors
- **Main Font**: Montserrat (Thin/Light weights: 300-400).
- **Header Tracking**: High tracking (0.5em) for all-caps titles to create "breathing room."
- **Color Palette**: 
  - #000000 (Black)
  - #FFFFFF (White)
  - #001F3F (Estate Navy)

## 4. Key Value Propositions (Offers)
- **Seller Credit**: $2,000 credit toward seller closing costs (Register + List).
- **Buyer Assistance**: $700 credit toward home warranty or inspection services.
- **Forbes Reach**: Highlight the "Invitation-only network" with 140M+ monthly visitors across 26+ countries.

## 5. Layout & Functional Requirements
- **Hero Section**: Full-screen cinematic property background with large-scale serif or thin sans-serif typography.
- **Social Connect**: Unified icons for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
- **Responsiveness**: Ensure the "Luxury" feel scales to mobile (no overcrowding, preserve whitespace).
- **Legal Disclaimer**: Mandatory Forbes trademark disclaimer at the bottom of all marketing materials.
